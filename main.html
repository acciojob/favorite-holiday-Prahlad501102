Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station located in Himachal Pradesh, India. It is known for its scenic mountains, pleasant weather, adventure sports, and peaceful environment, making it a perfect holiday destination.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley – Famous for adventure sports
  2. Rohtang Pass – Known for snow and breathtaking views
  3. Hadimba Temple – A historic wooden temple
  4. Old Manali – Popular for cafes and culture
  5. Vashisht Hot Springs – Natural hot water springs

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – Traditional Himachali bread
  • Thukpa – A popular noodle soup
  • Trout Fish – Fresh river fish delicacy
  • Momoes – Steamed dumplings
  • Mittha – A sweet local dessert
