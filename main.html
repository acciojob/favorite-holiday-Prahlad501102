Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station located in Himachal Pradesh, India. It is known for its scenic mountains, pleasant weather, adventure sports, and peaceful environment, making it a perfect holiday destination.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley
  2. Rohtang Pass
  3. Hadimba Devi Temple
  4. Old Manali
  5. Beas River

Must-try Foods

  • Siddu
  • Thukpa
  • Trout Fish
  • Babru
